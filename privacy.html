Privacy Policy

Welcome to ZapX, the app designed to help you find photographers and arrange photoshoots. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the controls we provide you to manage your information.

This Privacy Policy is part of our User Agreement, which includes our Terms of Service and Community Standards. By using ZapX, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data You Provide to Us

Account Creation Data: To create an account, you need to provide your phone number, first and last name, date of birth, and a username. You may also choose to provide a biography, email address, location, and profile picture.

Content Data: This includes photos, videos, messages, captions, comments, and geolocation data that you create and share on ZapX.

Communication Data: When you contact us for support, respond to surveys, or report issues, we collect the content of your communications, including any photos or identifiers you provide.

1.2 Data We Collect When You Use Our Services

Connection and Technical Data: Information such as your IP address, account creation date, last login, and device information.

Interaction Data: Details about your interactions with other users, including friends, friend invitations, comments, and reactions.

Usage Data: Actions performed on the app, such as posting, viewing content, and interacting with features.

Device and Equipment Data: Information about your device type, operating system, language preference, and app usage.

Geolocation Data: If you choose to share your location when posting content, we collect precise or approximate location data.

Camera and Photo Library Access: To use certain features, you may grant us access to your device’s camera and photo library.

2. How We Use Your Information

To Provide and Improve Our Services: To create your account, display content, and enhance your user experience.

Safety and Security: To protect users and ensure the security of our services.

Analytics and Measurement: To analyze app performance and usage for improvement and recommendations.

Communication: To communicate with you about app features, support requests, and updates.

Research: For feedback and product testing purposes, with your consent.

3. When We Share Your Information

With ZapX Employees: Authorized employees may access your information to provide and support our services.

With Other Users: Content you share may be visible to other users, depending on your privacy settings.

With Service Providers: We may share your information with partners who assist us in providing technical support, hosting, notifications, and analytics.

Legal Requirements: We may share information as required by law or to address security concerns.

In Case of Change in Ownership: In the event of a merger

4. Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control: You can view and update your account information and privacy settings at any time.

Data Portability: You have the right to request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and personal data. Some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

Opt-Out: You can opt-out of certain data collection practices, such as analytics and marketing communications.

Withdrawal of Consent: Where we rely on your consent to process data, you have the right to withdraw that consent at any time.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet-based service can be 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.
